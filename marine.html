About Marine Drive

Marine Drive is a 3.6 km-long boulevard along the Arabian Sea, famous for its C-shaped curve that resembles a necklace when illuminated at night. It is a popular destination for locals and tourists alike.

History

Built in the 1920s and 1930s, Marine Drive was part of Mumbai’s coastal development. The Art Deco buildings along the road have earned it a UNESCO World Heritage Site designation.
